# Prevention Checklist (Digital Safety)

## Account Security
- Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers and symbols) 
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
- Do not share OTP with anyone
- Change passwords regularly
- Use different passwords for different accounts


## Safe Internet Browsing
- Check website URL before login
- Look for https and lock icon
- Avoid clicking unknown links
- Do not download from untrusted sites
- Close suspicious pop-ups immediately


## Social Media Safety
- Keep profile private
- Accept requests from only known people
- Avoid posting personal information
- Think before sharing photos/videos
- Report fake or suspicious accounts


## Device Protection
- Install antivirus software
- Keep apps and system updated
- Use screen lock (PIN/password)
- Avoid using unknown USB devices
- Backup important data regularly


## Online Communication
- Do not reply to unknown messages
- Never share personal details online
- Avoid online arguments or bullying
- Verify before forwarding messages
- Block and report suspicious users


## Public Wifi Safety
- Turn off auto-connect options
- Use secure networks only
- Log out after using accounts


## UPI Transactions
- Avoid using public networks while online transactions
- Monitor your account regularly
- Do not enter your pin while people can see
- Never share details like PINs and bank passwords


## Online Banking
- Make a secure password
- Avoid public Wifi
- Do not buy things from sketchy sites
- Do not share credit card number online
